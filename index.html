The Perfect Boyfriend Jeans

Finding the perfect pair of jeans is still impossible for many women out there. Common problems such as having too much room at the waist, not enough room at the thighs or the existence of annoying ‘love handles’ can all be sources of frustration.
Tired of skinny jeans, but not quite ready for bell bottoms? Let’s take a trip back in time so we can introduce you to one of our favorite silhouettes: the boyfriend jean. Comfortable and casual, boyfriend jeans are loved for their easy-to-wear versatility. Whether you’re dressing them down with a graphic tee and cool sneaks, or going for a more elevated look with killer heels and a pair of statement earrings, there’s nowhere you can’t wear boyfriend jeans. The even better news? Not only are they available in a range of denim fabrics, but they’re also available in a range of prices. From $20 to $200, the perfect pair of boyfriend jeans is attainable for every budget.
Not to mention, each pair comes with its own unique details, like copper stitching, raw hems, pre-cuffed bottoms, and more. And with our list of Top 10 best boyfriend jeans, you’re sure to fall in love with at least one pair.
